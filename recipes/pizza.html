Pizza

Description

This simple brick-oven pizza recipe has been made famous by several well-known, Brooklyn wood-fire pizzerias. Best accompanied by ice-cold, pilsner-style lager beer.

Ingridients

  • Pasta Dought
  • Cheese
  • Tomato Souce

Steps

  1. Spread the pasta dought into a round shaped mattress
  2. add souce
  3. put in oven for 20 minutes
  4. add cheese and put back in oven to melt the cheese
