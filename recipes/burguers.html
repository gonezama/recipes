Burguers

Description

These wonderful cheese-stuffed burgers with bacon and caramelized onions are easy to adapt to suit different tastes. My kids, who normally don't have onions on their burgers, really enjoyed these. You can pan-fry them instead of grilling if you prefer.

Ingridients

  • Bread
  • Meat Medalion
  • Cheese
  • Souces (optional)

Steps

  1. Grill the meat

  2. When you are about to finish cooking the meat, add the cheese on top to melt

    Optionally you can toast the bread

  3. Put meat on the bread and add souces to taste
